At Sri Siddhi Diagnostics, we provide accurate and affordable diagnostic services with the latest equipment and expert staff. From blood tests to advanced imaging, we’re here to support your health journey with care and precision.

Do I need a doctor’s prescription for tests?

Most tests can be done without a prescription, but for specific diagnostics like 2D Echo or Ultrasound, a referral may be required. Feel free to call us for more details.

How will I receive my test reports?

Reports are available in both digital and printed formats. You can receive them via email, WhatsApp, or collect a hard copy from our center.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major payment methods including UPI, debit/credit cards, net banking, and cash. Our goal is to make your experience as smooth as possible.
